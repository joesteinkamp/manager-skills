## Starter Example

Below is a concrete example of completed objectives for a mid-level designer. Use as a quality reference.

### Report Context

- **Name:** Sarah
- **Role & level:** Product Designer, IC3 (Mid)
- **Team:** Growth team (B2B SaaS)
- **Tenure:** 14 months
- **Cycle:** Q2 2026
- **Career aspirations:** Grow into senior designer role by end of year. Wants to improve stakeholder communication and systems thinking.
- **Prior cycle highlights:** Strong craft execution. Shipped checkout redesign with measurable impact. Development area: tends to jump to solutions before framing the problem, and stakeholder presentations could be stronger.

### Objective 1: [Delivery] — Ship Dashboard v2 discovery and design spec

**Category:** Delivery
**Aligned to:** Team priority: "Launch Dashboard v2 MVP by Q3"

| Key Result | Evidence Type | Target | Status |
|------------|--------------|--------|--------|
| KR1: Complete discovery with 8+ user interviews synthesized into personas and journey map | Artifact | Research synthesis doc + persona cards delivered by April 15 | not started |
| KR2: Deliver design spec with full state coverage reviewed and approved by eng lead | Artifact | Spec covers all 5 core screens with default/empty/loading/error states, accepted by eng | not started |
| KR3: Define success metrics for Dashboard v2 before design begins | Artifact | Metrics framework with primary + guardrail metrics, reviewed by PM | not started |

### Objective 2: [Craft] — Improve problem framing before solution exploration

**Category:** Craft
**Aligned to:** Prior feedback: "jumps to solutions before framing the problem"

| Key Result | Evidence Type | Target | Status |
|------------|--------------|--------|--------|
| KR1: Every design spec starts with a problem statement that cites user evidence | Observation | Manager confirms problem framing quality in design reviews for all Q2 specs | not started |
| KR2: Present 2+ problem framings to the team before showing solutions in at least 2 projects | Observation | Manager observes in design reviews | not started |

### Objective 3: [Growth] — Build stakeholder communication skills

**Category:** Growth
**Aligned to:** Career aspiration: senior designer requires confident stakeholder communication

| Key Result | Evidence Type | Target | Status |
|------------|--------------|--------|--------|
| KR1: Lead 2 stakeholder presentations independently (without manager presenting) | Artifact + Feedback | Presentations delivered; stakeholder feedback collected | not started |
| KR2: Receive "confident and clear" or equivalent positive feedback from at least 1 stakeholder | Feedback | Written or verbal feedback captured | not started |

### Development Actions

- **Action 1:** Sarah will lead the Dashboard v2 mid-point stakeholder review in May.
  - By when: May 15
  - What the manager will provide: Dry-run rehearsal 2 days before, real-time feedback after, and `$stakeholder-presentation-writer` output as a starting structure.
- **Action 2:** Sarah will shadow Jordan's VP design review in April and debrief afterward.
  - By when: April 30
  - What the manager will provide: Pre-meeting context on what to observe, 30-minute debrief after.

### Manager Commitments

1. Share checkout post-launch metrics with Sarah by March 28 so she can reference impact in her promotion narrative.
2. Provide written feedback within 48 hours after each stakeholder presentation Sarah leads.
3. Ensure Sarah has 2 uninterrupted days per week for Dashboard v2 discovery (protect calendar).
