## Starter Example

Below is a concrete example of a completed mid-cycle check-in. Use as a quality reference.

### Tracking Context

- **Report:** Sarah (Product Designer, IC3)
- **Cycle:** Q2 2026
- **Cycle position:** Week 7 of 13 — 54% through cycle
- **Last check-in:** Week 4
- **Context changes:** Dashboard v2 scope expanded to include a mobile view (added by PM in Week 5). No team changes.

### Progress Dashboard

| Objective | Category | Status | Progress | Trend |
|-----------|----------|--------|----------|-------|
| Ship Dashboard v2 discovery and design spec | Delivery | 🟢 On track | 70% | → Steady |
| Improve problem framing before solution exploration | Craft | 🟢 On track | 67% | ↑ Improving |
| Build stakeholder communication skills | Growth | 🟡 Needs attention | 33% | → Flat |

**Overall:** Strong on delivery and craft. Growth objective at risk of falling behind — only 1 of 2 stakeholder presentations completed with 6 weeks remaining.

### Objective 3: Build stakeholder communication skills

**Status:** 🟡 Needs attention
**Category:** Growth

| Key Result | Status | Evidence | Progress |
|------------|--------|----------|----------|
| KR1: Lead 2 stakeholder presentations independently | 🟡 | Led 1 presentation (Dashboard v2 kickoff, April 8). Second presentation not yet scheduled. 6 weeks remain. | 1 of 2 (50%) |
| KR2: Receive positive stakeholder feedback on clarity | 🟢 | VP of Product commented "clear and well-structured" after April 8 presentation (Slack message, screenshot saved). | 1 of 1 (100%) |

**Assessment:** Sarah nailed her first presentation — the VP feedback was strong and specific. But she hasn't scheduled the second one, and the available opportunities are shrinking. The Dashboard v2 mid-point review (Week 10) is the natural next opportunity. If she misses that, she'll need to create an opportunity outside the normal cadence.

**Change since last check-in:** KR2 improved (achieved). KR1 flat (no new presentation since Week 4 check-in).

### Recommended Actions

**For Sarah:**
1. Schedule the Dashboard v2 mid-point stakeholder review for Week 10. Confirm with PM by end of this week.
   - Priority: This week
   - Why: Only 2 natural presentation opportunities remain in the cycle.

**For Jordan (Manager):**
1. Offer a dry-run session for Sarah's Week 10 presentation (as committed in objective-setting).
   - Priority: Week 9
   - Why: Manager commitment from objective-setting — the dry-run was specifically promised.

### Manager Commitment Status

| Commitment | Due | Status | Notes |
|-----------|-----|--------|-------|
| Share checkout post-launch metrics with Sarah | Mar 28 | ✅ Done | Shared in Week 5 1:1 |
| Written feedback within 48h after each presentation | Ongoing | ✅ Done (1 of 1) | Slack message sent April 9 |
| Protect 2 uninterrupted days/week for Dashboard v2 | Ongoing | 🟡 Partial | Week 6 had meeting conflicts on both days — address with Sarah |
